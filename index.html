Welcome to parking mapper

The goal of this web app is to easily add parking type by looking to aerial imagery.

Before starting to identify parking type, please have a look to the documentation to understand the differences between the main parking types.

You are connected as . Click on 'Start' to solve challenge or 'Logout' to change account.

When ready, press 'Authenticate' button.

Move the map to desired location, then press the button to get parkings.
